Tardigrade

Tardigrades are considered to be able to survive even complete global mass extinction events...

  • Scientific Name: Milnesium tardigradum
  • Average Length: 0.3-0.5 mm
  • Average Lifespan: Few months
  • Habitat: Globally widespread

Tardigrades (/ˈtɑːrdɪˌɡreɪd/; also known colloquially as water bears) are water-dwelling, eight-legged, segmented micro-animals.They were first discovered by the German zoologist Johann August Ephraim Goeze in 1773.
